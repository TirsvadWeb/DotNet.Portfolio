# Risk analysis
This risk analysis summarizes the main risks for the project divided by category.
Each risk has a unique ID, a rating of likelihood (1-5), severity (1-5), a calculated risk score (Likelihood × Severity), and recommended remedial actions.

## Risk level Matrix

| Score Range | Level |
|------------:|:-----:|
| 15–25 | Critical |
| 10–14 | High |
| 5–9 | Moderate |
| 1–4 | Low |
